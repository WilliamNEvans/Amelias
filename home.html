About

Amelia's is a nonprofit art gallery and music space. Amelia's provides an all inclusive space for artists to create and show their work. The goal is to help young San Francisco artists grow together and celebrate each others differences with art.There are many different forms of art that are showcased. Art forms include video, performance, painting, music, and more!

To learn more, volunteer, participate in a show, contribute financially, or donate art supplies, please contact Amelia at ameliameyerjane@gmail.com. Any support is appreciated!
